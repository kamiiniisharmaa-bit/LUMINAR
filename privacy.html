Privacy & Data Ethics

Last updated: June 11, 2026 · LUMINAIR Studio

Our Promise: Quiet Respect for Your Data

At LUMINAIR, we believe that privacy is a form of tenderness. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website luminair.studio (the “Site”), purchase our handcrafted objects, or interact with our brand. We are committed to maintaining the trust and confidentiality of everyone who enters our luminous space.

By using our Site, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide a seamless, artisanal experience, improve our offerings, and respect your preferences. The categories include:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, shipping/billing address, phone number (when you place an order or sign up for our journal).
  • Transaction Data: Products purchased, payment method details (handled via secure payment gateways — we never store full credit card numbers), order history.
  • Technical & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages visited, time spent on our Site, and referring URLs (collected via cookies and similar technologies).
  • Communications: Any correspondence you send to us, such as support requests, waitlist inquiries, or feedback.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us shape the LUMINAIR experience with intention and care. We use data for the following purposes:

  • Order Fulfillment: Process and ship your purchases, send order confirmations, and provide customer support.
  • Brand Communications: With your consent, send you our “Luminairessence” newsletter, early access to collections, and studio updates.
  • Service Improvement: Analyze how visitors interact with our Site to enhance design, product curation, and overall user experience.
  • Legal & Security: Detect and prevent fraud, enforce our Terms of Service, and comply with applicable laws.

3. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use minimal, respectful cookies to remember your preferences, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Below are the types of cookies we use:

🔹 Essential Cookies: Required for shopping cart, checkout, and security. Cannot be disabled.

🔹 Preference Cookies: Remember language or currency choices (if any).

🔹 Analytics Cookies: Anonymous traffic data (via tools like privacy-friendly analytics) to improve our site.

🔹 Marketing Cookies: Only with your explicit consent, we may use limited retargeting for our own campaigns.

By continuing to browse LUMINAIR, you accept our use of essential and preference cookies. For third-party analytics, we anonymize IP addresses.

4. Sharing & Disclosure of Data

We never sell your personal data. We only share information with trusted third parties who help us operate our business, subject to strict confidentiality agreements:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal), shipping carriers (DHL, FedEx, local postal partners), and email service providers for newsletters.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or governmental request, we may disclose information to protect our rights or the safety of others.
  • Business Transitions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred. We will notify you via email or prominent notice on our Site.

All our partners adhere to industry-standard security practices and are GDPR/CCPA compliant where applicable.

5. Data Security & Retention

We treat your data with the same care we give to our handcrafted objects. We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards — including SSL encryption, restricted access, and regular security reviews — to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. For example, order records are kept for 7 years for tax compliance, while newsletter subscription data is retained until you unsubscribe.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., residents of the EU, UK, California, or other regions), you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access & Portability: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Restriction & Objection: Limit how we process your data or object to certain uses (e.g., direct marketing).
  • Withdraw Consent: For processing based on consent (e.g., newsletters), you may withdraw at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@luminair.studio. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7. Children's Privacy

LUMINAIR does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 16. Our products and content are directed toward adults. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

8. Third-Party Links & Embedded Content

Our Site may contain links to external websites (e.g., payment gateways, social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before interacting with them.

9. International Data Transfers

LUMINAIR operates globally. Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place to protect your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

10. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may revise this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date indicates when changes were made. Material updates will be notified via a banner on our Site or by email if you are a registered user. Your continued use of LUMINAIR after changes signifies acceptance of the revised policy.
